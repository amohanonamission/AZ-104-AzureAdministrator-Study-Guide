ExamTips.md

I'd include things most people learn the hard way:

Before the Exam
Finish at least 5–10 practice exams.
Review your incorrect answers more than your correct ones.
Spend time in the Azure Portal.
Don't neglect Microsoft Learn for topics you consistently miss.
During the Exam
Read the last sentence of each question first.
Identify keywords like:
least administrative effort
lowest cost
most secure
high availability
business continuity
Eliminate obviously incorrect answers before choosing.
Flag difficult questions and return later if needed.
Think like an Azure Administrator—not just someone who memorized Azure services.
Most Common Comparisons
RBAC vs Azure Policy
Service Endpoint vs Private Endpoint
VPN Gateway vs ExpressRoute
Load Balancer vs Application Gateway
Traffic Manager vs Front Door
Azure Backup vs Site Recovery
Availability Sets vs Availability Zones
Azure Files vs Blob Storage
LRS vs ZRS vs GRS vs RA-GRS
Final Thought

Azure administration isn't about memorizing every service or setting. It's about understanding how Microsoft's cloud platform is designed, selecting the right service for each scenario, and continually building hands-on experience. Every deployment, lab, and troubleshooting session makes you a better administrator. Keep building, keep experimenting, and keep learning.
